Team

Braedan Mitchell

The man who started it all! Braedan Mitchell is the the lead founder of Soul Purpose Games, as well as the lead developer and storywriter for many games like The Genisis initiative and Father of mine. Braedan is a christian who gained a passion for video games at a young age, yet only decided to pursue game development as a career in his early 20s. After years of hard work and dedication, The Genisis Initiative was the first game to be made under the Soul Purpose Games banner, and has since been followed up with many more games in the works. Braedan's vision for Soul Purpose Games is to create games that not only entertain, but also get player to think about deep themes and ideas by creating immersive stories that anyone can get in on.

Azali

The wonderful Azali is the talented musical artist behind many soundtracks in Soul Purpose Games. Braedan found Azalis music online and reached out to see if his talents could be used to create an immersive soundtrack for The Genisis Initiative. Azali's music is known for its poweful chord arrangements, clever instrumeltal uses, and memorabel melodies that wrap you into the game's atmosphere. Azali has since worked on many more soundtracks for Soul Purpose Games, and continues to be a vital part of the team. If you want to listen to more of his music you can click here to visit his Spotify!

Isaiah Munoz

The amazing Isaiah Munoz is the lead artist and graphic designer for Soul Purpose Games. Isaiah's artistic talents have been a huge asset to the team, as he has created many of the game's character designs, environments, and promotional materials. Braedan first met Isaiah in church in their high school years, and they quickly bonded over their shared humor, love for games, and passion for the imagination. Isaiah has since become a close friend and trusted collaborator for Braedan, and his artistic vision has helped shape the visual identity of Soul Purpose Games.
